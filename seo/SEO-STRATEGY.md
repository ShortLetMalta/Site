## Market Context

- Malta short-let market generates ~**€47M annually**
- Valletta: 633 listings, 85% occupancy, €132 ADR, ~€39k/yr gross
- Sliema: 1,161 listings (highest supply), top quartile earns €194+/night
- St. Julian's: 80% occupancy, €133 ADR, ~€35k/yr gross
- Professional management delivers **30–50% revenue uplift** vs owner-managed
- **Legal Notice 92/2026** (May 2026): most comprehensive STR overhaul yet — platforms must verify MTA licence numbers and share monthly data with authorities; unlicensed operators face 3-year disqualification
- AI Overviews appear in only ~0.14% of local keywords — local SEO faces minimal AI disruption

## Strategic Positioning

Eleva's 30% fee is above-market. SEO content must justify the premium through:
1. **Results** — concrete owner revenue outcomes with location-specific data
2. **Compliance confidence** — MTA licensing, 2026 regulations, eco-tax, VAT handled
3. **Investor angle** — content that reaches buyers *before* they own a property
4. **AI-readiness** — structured content for ChatGPT/Perplexity local recommendations
