The Regulation

What Is an MTA Holiday Licence
and Who Needs One?

The Holiday Furnished Premises Licence is issued by the Malta Tourism Authority (MTA) and is required for any property rented to tourists for periods under 3 months. This includes Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, and any direct short-let rental.

🏠
You rent or list your property short-term

Any property rented for tourism purposes for less than 3 consecutive months requires a licence — no exceptions.

🌍
You live abroad and rent through an agent

Overseas owners using a management company still need the licence. The owner applies; we manage the entire process on your behalf.

🔑
You're about to list a new property

Getting the licence before the first booking avoids any gap period and protects your listing from day one.
