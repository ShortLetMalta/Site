FAQ

Sliema Short Let Questions

How much can I earn from a short let in Sliema?
Based on BnbCalc comparable data (May 2026): a 1-bedroom Sliema apartment grosses approximately €2,160/month at 68% occupancy and a €104 nightly rate. After Eleva's 30% management fee, owner net is around €1,510/month. A 2-bedroom earns ~€1,920/month net. These are market-rate comparables — actual results vary by spec, floor, and amenities.
Do I need a licence to short let my Sliema property?
Yes. All Malta short lets require an MTA Holiday Furnished Premises (HFP) licence. Eleva handles the full application and annual renewal as part of the management service.
How long does it take to get started in Sliema?
From initial assessment to first guest is typically 3–4 weeks, including MTA licence application, professional photography, and listing creation across all platforms.
What is Eleva's management fee for Sliema?
30% of rental income, net of OTA platform fees (Airbnb/Booking.com take their cut first, then we take 30% of what remains). No setup fee, no hidden charges, no lock-in.
